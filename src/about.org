** Projects
Some of the projects I am proud of:

- [[https://github.com/Martinsos/edlib][edlib]] - C/C++ library for sequence alignment I wrote for master's thesis that beyond my expectations became one of the foundational tools in bioinformatics.
- [[https://github.com/wasp-lang/strong-paths][strong-path]] - Library for (very) strongly typed paths in Haskell.
- [[https://github.com/Martinsos/hchess][hchess]] - My never-ending exploration of implementing chess in Haskell.
- [[./posts/building-my-first-keyboard.org][Mechanical keyboard]] I made from scratch (mostly).
